Pay Yourself First, Always

Automate a transfer to savings the moment you're paid, before a single bill goes out. Treating savings like a non-negotiable expense builds a cushion fast, cuts money stress, and makes every other habit on this list easier to keep.

Track Every Single Dollar

You can't fix what you don't see. Spend fifteen minutes a week reviewing where your money actually went, not where you meant it to go. Awareness alone cuts impulse spending, reveals sneaky subscriptions, and keeps your budget honest.

Kill High-Interest Debt Fast

Interest compounds against you just as fast as it compounds for you. List every balance, attack the highest rate first, and keep paying the minimum on the rest. Every dollar of interest avoided is a guaranteed return no market can promise.
