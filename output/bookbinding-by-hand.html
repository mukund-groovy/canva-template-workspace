Fold Every Signature With Care

Sew a Stitch That Holds

A simple Coptic or pamphlet stitch, pulled snug and even, will outlast a spine full of glue — learn the tension before you learn the pattern.

Trim With a Steady, Slow Hand

Score twice, cut once. A board shear rewards patience — a wobbly line is the one mistake bookcloth can never hide.

Let the Glue Cure Before You Peek

Press it under weight and walk away. A book opened too soon will warp — the wait is doing as much work as your hands did.
