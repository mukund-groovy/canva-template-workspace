A 5-Minute Study Guide

5 Study Habits That Stick

Real techniques, backed by memory science

Tip01

Space Out Your Reviews

Cramming fades by tomorrow. Revisit the same idea after a day, a week, then a month — and watch it stick.

Tip02

Teach It to Someone Else

If you can't explain it simply, you don't know it yet. Say it out loud, even to an empty room.

Tip03

Recall Beats Rereading

Highlighting feels like learning. Recalling is learning. Close the book and write what you actually remember.

Tip04

Focus on One Task Only

Every switch costs you focus. Silence the tabs, pick one task, and give it your full fifteen minutes.

Tip05

Sleep Locks in the Learning

Memory consolidates while you rest. A late night of cramming loses to an early night of sleep.
