7-Day Sourdough Ritual

HEARTHLOAF
Day 01

Feed your starter

Every morning, feed your starter equal parts flour and water. A bubbly, doubled jar by afternoon means it's strong enough to leaven your loaf.

02 / 08
HEARTHLOAF
Day 02

Mix, then let it rest

Combine flour, water, and salt, then walk away. A slow rest lets the gluten build itself, so you barely have to knead at all.

03 / 08
HEARTHLOAF
Day 03

Fold with patience

A few gentle stretch-and-folds every half hour build real strength into the dough. Handle it like it matters, and the crumb will thank you.

04 / 08
HEARTHLOAF
Day 04

Shape a tight boule

Tuck the edges under and drag the dough against the counter until the surface turns taut. Tension now becomes lift in the oven.

05 / 08
HEARTHLOAF
Day 05

Score before it bakes

A confident blade cut gives steam somewhere to go. Score deep and quick, right before the loaf meets the heat, for a clean, dramatic bloom.

06 / 08
HEARTHLOAF
Day 06

Bake low, slow, covered

A hot dutch oven traps steam around the crust, so the loaf can spring before it sets. Uncover near the end for a deep, burnished crust.

07 / 08
HEARTHLOAF
Day 07

Rest before you slice

Let the loaf cool for a full hour before cutting. The crumb is still setting inside, and an early slice steals the texture you worked all week for.
