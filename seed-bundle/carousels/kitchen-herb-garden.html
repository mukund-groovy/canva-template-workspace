Pick a Sunny Spot

Most cooking herbs want six hours of bright light a day. A south-facing sill or a sunny patio corner keeps basil, thyme and mint content all season long.

Water Only When Dry

Overwatering kills more herbs than drought ever does. Press a finger an inch into the soil — dry means pour, damp means wait one more day.
