Tip 02

Set A Ten-Minute Timer

Draw in short, low-pressure bursts instead of waiting for a free afternoon — a ten-minute cap makes starting easy.

Tip 03

Draw What's In Front Of You

Skip reference photos and sketch your coffee cup, your hands, your room — ordinary objects teach observation fastest.

Tip 04

Keep Every Page, Even Bad Ones

Resist tearing out sketches that didn't turn out right — a messy sketchbook is proof of practice, not something to hide.
