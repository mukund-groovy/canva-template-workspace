Set the

Habit 01

Light candles early, dim the overheads, and let the music arrive before your guests do. A room should feel warm before anyone walks in.

03 / 05 Hearth & Co. Hosting

Feed Them
First

02
Habit 02

Have something in hand within five minutes of arrival — a snack, a drink, anything at all. Nobody relaxes on an empty stomach.

Leave Space

03
Habit 03

Skip the tight schedule and the timed courses. The best part of any gathering is the long stretch nobody is rushing through.

04
Habit 04

How a night ends stays with people longer than how it started. Walk them out, say their name, and mean the goodbye.
